Florida Historic Capitol Museum

This museum is a treasure trove of Florida's political history, showcasing artifacts and exhibits that tell the story of the state's legislative past. Visitors can explore the beautifully restored chambers, learn about the evolution of Florida's government, and even see how the Capitol has changed over the years.
